Cover designed for tradies

This Trades Insurance offer has been developed by experts so you can pick the right insurance package for your business. With the flexibility to choose what you need, this is the easiest way to get insurance online.

CGU Trades Insurance covers you for:

Liability Insurance

CGU's liability insurance helps you get on site and protect your business. CGU’s Liability Insurance is designed to protect your business against liability claims resulting from accidents or injuries that occur as result of your business activities, and accidental damage to property owned or controlled by someone else.

Our trades cover lets you choose the level of liability cover that works for your business.

For the full list of policy inclusions and exclusions, download the Policy Wording.

Tool Cover

Flexible Tool Cover that keeps you in business.

We understand that without your tools you can’t work. If they get stolen, damaged or lost we will get them replaced so you can get back to work asap.

Our tool cover lets you:

  • Select the right level of tool cover for your business
  • Add tool coverage for overnight storage on site
  • Add Portable Electronic Equipment cover for devices such as mobiles, tablets and laptops.

For the full list of policy inclusions and exclusions, download the Policy Wording.

Portable Electronic Equipment Cover

This covers your Portable Electronic Equipment such as mobiles, tablets, laptops if they are stolen, damaged or lost.

For the full list of policy inclusions and exclusions, download the Policy Wording.

Commercial Motor Vehicle Insurance

Insurance to cover your work vehicle and trailer. Pick and choose what cover will suit you best and purchase online today.

For the full list of policy inclusions and exclusions, download the Policy Wording.

Case study:
Mike the Electrician

Mike was on the job at a customer’s house when a visitor tripped over and injured themselves on Mike’s tools he’d left in the driveway. The visitor sued Mike and he was ultimately held liable for the person’s injuries.

Show Mike's story

Luckily, the General and Products Liability section of Mike’s CGU Trades Insurance Policy meant he was covered for his legal liability to pay compensation for Personal Injury. It included the amount awarded to the third party and coverage for CGU’s costs to defend Mike against the claim.

These costs add up and without Liability Cover, the impact of Mike’s legal costs and court awards could have put him out of business.
