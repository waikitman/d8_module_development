Update: CGU Private Motor Policy

Cover Current As of 1st August 2017

Extra costs of Reinstatement

If personal items are damaged in an incident or stolen from your locked car, we will cover the cost to repair or replace them, up to $500 (inclusive of GST).

We will only cover loss or damage as a result of theft or attempted theft from a locked car.

The theft or attempted theft of personal items must be reported to the Police.

We will not cover:
*money, cheques, financial transaction cards, property used for earning an income.

If personal items are damaged in an incident or stolen from your locked car, we will cover the cost to repair or replace them, up to $1,000 (inclusive of GST).

We will only cover loss or damage as a result of theft or attempted theft from a locked car.

The theft or attempted theft of personal items must be reported to the Police.

We will not cover:
*money, cheques, financial transaction cards, property used for earning an income.

Motorcycle Helmets and Protective Clothing

If your helmet or any other protective clothing is stolen from your motorcycle or damaged in an incident, we will pay for the cost to replace them, up to $250 (inclusive of GST).

If your helmet or any other protective clothing is stolen from your motorcycle or damaged in an incident, we will pay for the cost to replace them, up to $2,000 (inclusive of GST).

The most we will pay for any one item of protective clothing is $1,000.

Emergency or Temporary Repairs

If your vehicle needs emergency or temporary repairs so that it can be driven or used, we will cover the cost of repairs, up to $500 (inclusive of GST)

The emergency or temporary repairs must be a result of an incident we have agreed to cover.

We will need receipts of the repairs.

If your vehicle needs emergency or temporary repairs so that it can be driven or used, we will cover the cost of repairs, up to $800 (inclusive of GST)

The emergency or temporary repairs must be a result of an incident we have agreed to cover.

We will need receipts of the repairs.
