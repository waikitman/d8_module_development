We're here for you

Whether you need make a claim or to ask a question we're only a phone call away.

  • To make a claim

    You can call us on 13 24 80 (24 hours/7 days a week) or select one of the following areas:

    • Personal claims page
    • Business claims page
    • Workers Compensation page

  • Business insurance renewals and payments

    Visit the Business Insurance Renewals and Payments page

  • To request business insurance documentation

    • Complete the Certificate of Currency (COC) online form
    • Complete the Tax invoice online form
    • Complete the Certificate of Insurance online form

  • To request personal insurance documentation

    • Complete the Certificate of Currency (COC) online form
    • Complete the Tax invoice online form
    • Complete the Request Policy Schedule online form

  • To make changes/updates to your policy or personal details

    • Call 13 24 81 (8:30am to 5:00pm, Monday to Friday)
    • Or complete the Policy Change Online Form

  • General or business enquiries

    • Complete the General Enquiries Online Form
    • For business insurance enquiries, request a call back from a CGU business insurance specialist

To help us make the process easier, please have the following ready when you contact us:
