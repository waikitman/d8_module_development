Anchors away! Set sail with CGU Boat Insurance

CGU Boat Insurance provides comprehensive cover against theft, fire, accidental or malicious damage… even stranding and capsizing is covered. So you can sail away without a worry in the world.

  • New for old replacement

    If your boat is less than a year old and is a total loss, we’ll buy you a new one (with our Comprehensive cover).

  • Cover your marine accessories

    Up to $1,000 worth of cover for marine accessories, contents and tools that you take with you on the boat.

  • Enjoy all of Australia's waterways

    You’re covered while navigating inland waters, and coastal waters up to 200 nautical miles offshore.

  • Choose your optional extras

    From water skiing to yacht racing and extra accessories - it’s easy to add extra cover to your boat insurance.

  • Doing rescue work?

    Voluntary rescue work is covered. Personal property on board the boat is too.

  • $10 million liability cover

    If your boat causes damage to another person’s property, we’ll take care of it.
