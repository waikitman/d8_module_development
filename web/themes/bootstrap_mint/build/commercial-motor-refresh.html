Commercial Motor

CGU Commercial Motor Insurance enables all your different vehicles to be insured under one policy.

What protection will a Commercial Vehicle Insurance policy provide?

CGU's Commercial Motor Insurance enables all your different vehicles to be insured under the one policy, from cars, trailers and caravans to goods carryiong vehicles and mobile plant machinery. We're making insurance easier for you.

Key Features

  • Up to $30 million liability cover
  • Repair guarantee
  • Cover for hire car costs
  • Cover for payment to your financial provider
  • Choice of agreed value or market value for most types of sedans, station wagons, four wheel drives, utilities and vans with comprehensive cover

Vehicles over 5 tonnes

For commercial vehicles with a carrying capacity over 5 tonnes, we recommend National Transport Insurance (NTI), Australia's largest truck insurer. Visit the NTI website to learn more.
