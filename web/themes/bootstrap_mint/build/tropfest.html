CGU Tropfest announcement with IAG's Ben Bessell

Watch Ben Bessell, Commercial Insurance Chief Executive for IAG discuss how and why CGU Insurance is supporting Tropfest's return in 2016.

CGU HELPING TROPFEST SEE IT THROUGH

CGU Insurance partner with thousands of Australian small businesses to help them see it through – especially in times of need.

The collapse of Tropfest has been a financial disaster for many Australian small businesses so we’re now partnering with Tropfest to help these small businesses see it through.

Ben Bessell, Commercial Insurance Chief Executive for IAG, which owns the CGU business, said he was delighted to support this iconic event:

“Supporting this fantastic cultural event is about creating a renewed opportunity for Tropfest and all those small businesses associated with the event to continue to thrive and get some business done.”

“It’s also about supporting the wider community who are avid fans of film and entertainment and give them an opportunity to come along and enjoy a free cultural event.”
