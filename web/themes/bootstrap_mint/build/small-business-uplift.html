Uplift Modules

Insure your business vehicles or fleet under one policy with CGU commercial motor insurance.

Find an insurance broker

CGU’s Commercial Motor insurance keeps your business on the road with a cover to suit you. Our cover allows you to insure your different vehicles under one policy – from cars, trailers and caravans to goods carrying vehicles and mobile plant machinery.

Insure any number of vehicles

We insure a single business vehicle up to fleets of hundreds, and insure some of Australia’s largest commercial fleets.

Policy options

You can customise your policy by choosing between comprehensive, third party fire and/or theft or third party only for each vehicle you insure.

Get a quote

Our Commercial Motor and Motor Fleet insurance products are available through insurance brokers and authorised representatives. An insurance broker will give you advice to make sure you have the right cover for your business needs.

Find an insurance broker

  • Commercial Motor

    CGU’s Commercial Motor insurance covers up to 15 different vehicles under one policy – including cars, trailers, caravans, goods carrying vehicles and mobile plant machinery.

  • Motor Fleet

    CGU Motor Fleet insurance covers fleets over 15 vehicles, tailored to your business needs. Customers with CGU Motor Fleet insurance will get access to our safe driving and risk management tools, to help your drivers improve safety and reduce their risks.

  • Heavy Motor

    For Commercial Vehicles with a carrying capacity over 5 tonnes, we recommend insuring with National Transport Insurance (NTI), Australia’s largest truck insurer.

Workers compensation

  • CGU is one of Australia's leading providers

    CGU provides workers compensation insurance to more than 100,000 Australian businesses and is one of Australia's leading workers compensation providers.

  • We partner with your business

    We will partner with your business to reduce workplace injuries and achieve sustainable return to work outcomes. With a national service offering, your business receives great service no matter where you interact with us.

  • State by legislation

    CGU workers compensation offers tailored, innovative risk management products and services with premiums that encourage and reward workplace safety and positive claims outcomes

  • Workers compensation is determined by state-based legislation

    CGU offers workers compensation in all states and territories except Queensland and South Australia. For further information about workers compensation in these states visit WorkCover Queensland and ReturnTo WorkSA.

Public Liability insurance FAQs

  • What is public liability insurance?

    When you call us to lodge a claim, you’ll be asked to provide:

    • Your policy number or personal contact details, and the contact details of anyone involved in an incident, including their name, current address, vehicle registration details, phone number and insurance details if known
    • Any letters, notices or court documents about the incident, and the incident report number for any claims in relation to theft or attempted theft, vandalism or a malicious act (the police will provide you with this number when you report the incident to them).
