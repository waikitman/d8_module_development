It’s the little things that make our Buildings & Contents Insurance better

CGU’s combined Building & Contents Insurance is our most popular type of home insurance, giving you cover for both your home and the many things inside it. You’ve got three options for your Building & Contents Insurance.

  • Generous new-for-old replacement

    If the contents of your home are damaged or destroyed, we won’t ask you how old they were. Instead, we’ll arrange repairs or help you buy new replacements.

  • A roof over your head

    Worried about where you’ll sleep at night if your home is damaged? CGU looks after you, providing temporary accommodation and arranging emergency repairs.

  • Trusted network of repairers

    If you need to make emergency repairs to your home, trust CGU to get the job done. Our reliable network of repairers will fix it, fast.

  • We offer pay by the month at no extra cost

    Prefer paying your home insurance in monthly installments? Set up direct debit with us and pay for your home insurance by the month with no additional cost.

  • CGU flood cover

    CGU includes automatic flood cover on Home insurance. This ensures that all customers are covered for damage caused by water giving you peace of mind when water damage occurs.

Building & Contents Insurance FAQs

  • What is Building & Contents Insurance?

    When you take out Buildings & Contents Insurance, you’re taking out cover for both the home you live in (including your garage, shed and other structures on your property) plus its contents. It’s the most comprehensive type of home insurance you can take out.

    How much you’re covered for depends on a few things. You choose the sum insured for both your home and its contents, and then you can choose from three levels of cover – Premium, Quality or Basic.

    To really wrap your head around what’s covered with our Buildings & Contents Insurance, you should take a look at each Product Disclosure Statement (PDS):

    • Premium – Accidental Damage PDS
    • Quality – Listed Events PDS
    • Basic – Fundamentals PDS

  • How much does Building & Contents Insurance cost?

    When we calculate your home insurance premium, we look at a whole range of factors – from the materials used to build your home to its location. We even consider whether you’ve got an alarm system or not.

    The best way to answer this question is to complete a quick quote.

  • What isn't covered by Building & Contents Insurance?

    This depends on the type of policy you’ve taken out. Each of our Buildings & Contents Insurance policies comes with a Product Disclosure Statement (PDS), which lists out all the exclusions to your cover.

    We encourage you to read your PDS to see the full list of what is and isn’t covered:

    • Premium – Accidental Damage PDS
    • Quality – Listed Events PDS
    • Basic – Fundamentals PDS

  • Is Building & Contents Insurance best for me?

    Our Building & Contents Insurance is the most popular type of home insurance. Most people choose this comprehensive cover because, if your home is damaged in a weather event or other incident, then it’s pretty likely that your contents will be damaged too.

    Say, for example, your home is flooded. Not only do you need to repair or replace some of the walls (part of the building) but you will also need to repair or replace carpets, furniture and valuable items (all considered contents).

    Please read through these Product Disclosure Statements (PDSs) to get a better idea of whether Building & Contents Insurance is right for you:

    • Premium – Accidental Damage PDS
    • Quality – Listed Events PDS
    • Basic – Fundamentals PDS
