Careers

We want our people to the get the most out of their career at CGU.

We offer all our employees the opportunity to grow and develop to their full potential.

The employment experience we offer is defined by starting or building exceptional careers, development and an expectation from our employees on diversity, inclusion and flexibility; a positive culture based on values and fair reward for the work they do.

If you’re looking for that, then we’re looking for you.
