Benefits of having travel insurance

This might sound like your parents but, even though you're young, please take out travel insurance. You might think you're invincible, but what happens if you have an accident and how much will you be out of pocket? Having student travel insurance means you're covered for many unforeseen accidents and won’t wind up blowing your entire bank balance on hospital bills, which would be a major bummer. Be sure to read the PDS carefully to understand exactly what you’ll be covered for under your policy.

When you set out to take on the world, it's likely you'll be swimming, kayaking, and snorkelling on your trip. We can sort out your level of travel insurance to cover you when you're in the water, in case the unexpected happens. If an event occurs in which you are covered, we'll also cover you if you need to be medically evacuated.

Sometimes nature gets in the way of your plans. Your flight or organised trip might get delayed or cancelled due to storms, cyclones, you name it. If you don't have travel insurance, you'll end up paying for more accommodation and travel expenses, and waving bye-bye to the money set aside for that killer bungee jump, so get covered!

Remember, taking out travel insurance is for peace of mind, so you can enjoy yourself to the max and not panic about the what-ifs. We can even help you out if your tablet is stolen or if your luggage gets damaged in transit. So make sure you've organised insurance before you go. Got it? Good.
