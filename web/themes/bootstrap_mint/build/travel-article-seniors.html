Seniors travel insurance

As a global nomad about to take on Everest, cruise the Caribbean, 4WD around Australia, or play golf in Scotland, it's imperative you have travel insurance for your trip.

In this guide

As we get older and hit retirement, it's likely we'll have a few health issues – but these should not stop us from seeing the world. Get seniors travel insurance and you can also take the grandkids with you for free!*

Benefits of having seniors travel insurance

Taking out a travel insurance policy that's tailored to you will give you peace of mind. You might have a dodgy knee from playing 30-odd years of footy, or you might have a heart condition. Everyone is different – and everyone can apply for cover.

Some medical conditions are automatically covered, and others – like your knee – require additional cover. For some conditions, we'll ask you to fill out a medical assessment form, then, once you're approved, you'll have travel insurance for both overseas medical and dental treatment.

If you're 70 years or older, you have to do a medical assessment before we can approve your travel insurance. It's reassuring to know we also offer a great piece of cover for loved ones who aren't travelling with you, so you can get home to be with them if they are hospitalised or die.

If you're hiking, snorkelling or even diving on your trip, we'll look after you if the unexpected happens. Your holiday might be delayed or cancelled, but you can rest easy knowing that your accommodation and travel expenses are covered. Plus, taking out travel insurance means we can help you out if anything is stolen, or if your luggage gets damaged in transit.
