Our leadership

Meet our executive team at CGU.

Peter Harmer

Chief Executive Officer

Peter was appointed as CEO of CGU Insurance on 8 November 2010.

He began his career in the insurance industry at South British Insurance, which became part of the CGU group of companies in 1981. Since then, Peter's career has spanned underwriting, reinsurance broking and commercial insurance broking.

His most recent role was as CEO of Aon’s UK business. Prior to that, he was CEO of Aon’s Australian operations.

Stuart Chapman

Chief Financial Officer

Stuart was appointed CGU's Chief Financial Officer in January 2012. He is a Chartered Accountant with more than 15 years experience working in the insurance industry.

Stuart joined CGU through our Swann subsidiary in 2001 and held several senior management positions at Swann before being appointed Swann General Manager in 2007.

Prior to Swann, Stuart was Finance Manager at Fortis, which he joined after six years with Ernst & Young.

Ben Bessell

General Manager, Broker & Agency

Ben is the General Manager of Broker & Agency which is responsible for managing and developing CGU's relationships with insurance brokers and agents across Australia and CGU’s workers compensation business.

Prior to his current appointment, Ben was General Manager of CGU Workers Compensation and before that General Manager, CGU Claims.

Ben has almost 20 years’ experience working in the insurance sector. He has extensive experience working across underwritten insurance markets and with government-funded workers' compensation schemes.

He began employment with the Insurance Australia Group (IAG) in Tasmania in 1993 and has worked in a range of claims and management roles across Australia.

Ben is an ANZIIF member.

Donna Walker

BSc, FIAA
General Manager, Claims & Chief Actuary

Donna is the General Manager of Claims and Chief Actuary. Prior to her current role, Donna was General Manager of Technical Services.

Since joining CGU in 1999, Donna has been involved in a numerous areas of the business, including pricing frameworks, reinsurance projects, performance monitoring and financial reporting, information delivery and expense analysis.

Prior to joining CGU, Donna spent three years lecturing at the University of Melbourne in the Centre for Actuarial Studies and held various roles in the superannuation industry, managing client relationships and agreeing services and strategies for a portfolio of corporate clients.

Donna is actively involved with the Institute of Actuaries of Australia (IAAust), helping to establish new education programs. She served as a member of the IAAust Council for three years from 2000 - 2002.

Malcolm Freeman

ANZIIF (Associate)
General Manager, Business Partners

Malcolm is the General Manager of the Business Partners strategic distribution unit.

With over 25 years of insurance experience, he has held a number of senior positions with CGU, including two years leading the New South Wales Workers Compensation business, National Manager (Marine) and National Manager (Motor Trades).

His other roles have included working in strategy and project management.

Malcolm has international experience working with the insurance industry in Asia and the United Kingdom. He is a graduate of the Mt Eliza business management school and an associate member of ANZIIF.

Paul Ayton

General Manager, Swann Insurance

Paul became the General Manager of Swann Insurance in January 2012.

Paul has been a member of Swann’s Executive Team for more than eight years, and during this time has been responsible for strategy, corporate marketing, projects, operations and corporate relationships.

Swann Insurance is a niche general insurer, distributing its unique product range through more than 1,600 motor and motorcycle dealers. Swann’s products are sold under its own brand as well as the brands of many of Australia’s leading motor vehicle and motorcycle manufacturers, importers and financiers to the automotive industry.

Paul has more than 20 years' experience in financial services in various roles with CUNA Mutual Group, GE Finance, Hewlett Packard and Westpac.

Neil Whiteing

Chief Operating Officer

Neil is the Chief Operating Officer with overall responsibility for CGU’s business operations, business technology services, human resources and legal functions.

Neil joined CGU in September 2011 from within IAG where he was IAG’s Group CIO responsible for all shared technology services across the group.

Prior to joining IAG, Neil held numerous senior roles in technology at Charles Schwab & Co in San Francisco. He has an extensive consulting background working for Bain & Company in San Francisco as well as Deloitte Consulting in Australia.

He has an MBA from the Wharton School of Business and Bachelor of Commerce (Honours) from the University of Cape Town.

Phuong Ly

Acting Chief Underwriting Officer

Phuong is CGU’s Acting Chief Underwriting Officer. He has more than a decade of experience in senior underwriting roles, with particular emphasis on the construction and engineering sectors.

Prior to his latest appointment, Phuong was CGU’s National Manager – Construction and Engineering and before that he held key roles across the industry.

Phuong has a Graduate Diploma of Insurance at Deakin University and a Bachelor of Engineering (Chemical) and Bachelor of Commerce.

He is also a Fellowship member of the Australian and New Zealand Institute of Insurance and Finance (ANZIIF).

Lee Heycox

B.Arts (Psych & IR)
Executive General Manager, People Operations Australia

Lee is the Executive General Manager, People Operations Australia.

His 30 years of experience working in the human resources sector spans across a diverse range of industries.

Prior to his current role, Lee held several senior management positions including Executive Manager Human Resources - Personal Customers at St George Bank and as HR Director at The Age newspaper.

Lee holds a Bachelor of Arts (Psychology and Industrial Relations) and is a member of the Australian HR Institute.
